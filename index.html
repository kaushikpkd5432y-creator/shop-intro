Munna & Dharmadev Sha Miscellaneous Store

Serving the community since 1955

Established in 1955, Munna & Dharmadev Sha Miscellaneous Store has proudly stood as a trusted destination for the community for more than seven decades. The store was originally founded by Dharmadav Sah, father of Munna Sha, with a vision to provide people with all essential items under one roof. Today, the legacy continues under the dedicated management of Munna Sha, who carries forward the values of honesty, service, and tradition.

Known as a true miscellaneous hub, the store offers a wide variety of products to meet everyday needs. Customers can find household goods, daily essentials, and rare items that are often difficult to locate elsewhere. One of the unique specialties of the store is its collection of organic medicines, carefully chosen to promote health and wellness naturally. Alongside this, the store provides complete puja essentials, making it a one-stop destination for spiritual and cultural requirements.

Over the years, Munna & Dharmadev Sha Miscellaneous Store has become more than just a shop—it is a part of people’s lives. Generations of customers have trusted the store for quality, reliability, and warmth. With a strong commitment to customer satisfaction, the store continues to uphold the values set by its founder, ensuring that every visitor experiences convenience, tradition, and care in every purchase.
